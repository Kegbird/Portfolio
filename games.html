Spinning Tower - Unity 3D

Spinning Tower is a 3D tower defence where the protagonist, a mage, tries to grasp the meaning of life. In Spinning Tower, as the name suggests, there is a tower to defend: You will control the rotation (and fire) of an enchanted cannon which rotates on the tower axis. Wave of enemies constantly siege the tower and some of them can apply malus to the gameplay, making it more challenging. Power ups, dropped by killed enemies, will surely help the player to survive more time.

Disdice - Unity 3D

Brain Bound is 2d top down shooter, where the 6 faces die fights against other type of dice. The main character has 6 forms: each form has different stats and (eventually) weapons; the player can change form whenever wants, but he can't decide which form to use. This game was our entry for the Bay Area Jam.
